About me

I'm Nissa — a training facilitator and digital learning specialist based in Bandung, Indonesia, with 4+ years of experience designing and delivering engaging learning experiences across corporate, education, and community settings.

How to add your real Google Drive files

  1. Upload your document (PDF, Google Doc, Google Slides) to Google Drive.
  2. Right-click the file → Share → change to Anyone with the link can view.
  3. Click Copy link. The link looks like:
    https://drive.google.com/file/d/ABC123xyz/view
  4. In this HTML file, find the card for this project and replace YOUR_FILE_ID with ABC123xyz (the part between /d/ and /view).
  5. For Google Slides, the share link looks different — use the "Publish to web" → Embed option instead, and use the /embed URL it gives you.
  6. Save the file and push to GitHub. Done!
💡 Quick tip for Google Docs & Sheets
Change the end of the URL from /view to /preview for a clean embed without the Drive UI chrome. Example:
https://drive.google.com/file/d/ABC123/preview
